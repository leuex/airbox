About AIRBOX

Welcome to AIRBOX NPO

AIRBOX is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing free, high-quality coding education to anyone who wants to learn. Our mission is to break down barriers to tech education and empower individuals with the skills they need to succeed in the digital age.

Through our Zoom-based platform, we offer interactive coding classes taught by experienced instructors. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to enhance your skills, AIRBOX provides the resources and community to help you grow.
